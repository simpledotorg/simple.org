Contact us

The Simple project is funded by the not-for-profit Resolve to Save Lives (rtsl.org). The simplest way to contact us is via our contact form or send an email to info@resolvetosavelives.org.

To request to have your patient data or user account removed from Simple in your country, please email us with your information: help@simple.org.
