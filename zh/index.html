快速，免费的应用程序，临床医生追踪高血压患者。

Simple is designed to support large-scale hypertension and NCD management programs. Currently, the app is actively used in 1,685 public health facilities in India and Bangladesh to manage 447,014 patients (4-Jan-2021) with hypertension and diabetes.

Healthcare workers record every patient visit in an app, managers get daily reports to monitor progress, and patients can chart their own BPs and blood sugars.

1. Simple app

An easy-to-use mobile app for healthcare workers to record BPs, blood sugars, and medicines at every patient visit, in less than 20 seconds.

2. Simple dashboard

A web-based dashboard for officials and health system managers to monitor hypetension control across facilities and regions.

3. BP Passport app

An app for patients and caregivers to record BPs and blood sugars, which reminds patients to take their medications. iOS and Android.

Is Simple really free?

This project is supported by Resolve to Save Lives, which enables us to provide Simple completely free of cost with no strings attached. This project will always be open source — free and open to contributions from everyone.
