BP Passport

The world's simplest app to track your blood pressure, blood sugar, and remember to take your medicines.

Android app iPhone app

The Simple project makes a free easy-to-use BP Passport app for patients to track their own BPs, blood sugars, and medications.

Free for everyone

The BP Passport app is totally free and will be free forever.

Very simple

The BP Passport app is designed to be simple. It is easy to learn and easy to use, even for people who don't have much experience using smartphone applications.

Available in many languages

The BP Passport app is currently available in:

  • English
  • French
  • Hindi
  • Marathi
  • Punjabi
  • Spanish

Want your language? Please contact us.
